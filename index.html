Who We Are

Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

The vision of The Fighting Saints is to train as many 8–17 year olds to be successful both on the court and off the court. Through discussions, mentoring, and sweat equity, we redirect potentially risky youth behaviors by fostering self-awareness, goal-setting, and critical thinking.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to assist youth and their family in a process of finding high-quality coaching and mentoring with a focus on providing wraparound support services. We teach sports ethics and a healthy lifestyle, equipping athletes with tools to become successful adults in our community.

Meet the Founder

Karla Nash

CEO & Founder

Since founding the Fighting Saints in 2008, Karla Nash has dedicated herself to empowering San Diego's youth through basketball. Her vision goes beyond the court — it's about building character, discipline, and a pathway to success for every young athlete.

Under her leadership, the Fighting Saints have grown from a small community program into a competitive organization producing champions on the court and leaders in the community. Through pride, hard work, and forward-thinking attitudes, our athletes learn to succeed in every area of life.
